OUR MISSION

Our mission at Encounter Web Design is to provide you with web solutions to help connect your business with your customers. In today's world people first search online to find what they need. Without an engaging website, connecting your customers to you, those potential customers may be lost to competitors. We can help identify your needs and craft a custom solution to make sure that you are maximizing your online presence and potential business.
We can't wait to hear from you so we can plan your next project!


A large number of American businesses still do not have a website or have a web presence that is out of date. Our goal is to assist companies in getting online. Many do not know how to go about getting their business on the web. We will use your initial consultation to determine your needs and we will design a custom solution around your situation. This solution may involve an eCommerce option, online scheduling, ways to communicate with your customers, search engine optimization (SEO) and more. We will then generate a custom quote. After your new site is launched we are able to assist with hosting, ongoing maintenance, and more.
We pride ourselves on providing the highest level of service. We see ourselves as problem solvers and are dedicated to helping you reach your goals. We do not take a “one size fits all” approach as no two situations are the same.

We can build a starter “store front” website for as low as $500. Larger projects will be based on a custom quote. We will tailor a solution that is specific to your needs and will work within your budget.
